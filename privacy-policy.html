Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

AutoVibe Motors ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a contact form, request a test drive, or apply for financing. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Financial information for credit applications
  • Vehicle preferences and trade-in details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process your enquiries, facilitate vehicle sales, manage financing applications, and send you relevant marketing communications (with your consent).

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with banking partners for financing, insurance providers, and service providers who assist our operations. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect certain site functionalities.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law (e.g., for tax or legal reporting).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
